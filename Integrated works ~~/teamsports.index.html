What Are Team Sports ?

a team sport in which players compete against each other. "Baseball is a team sport, but golf is not," says the author. an energetic pastime that necessitates physical exertion and rivalry

There are many team sports however i will be focusing on the team sports such as :

  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Soccer

    Basketball

    - Basketball is defined as a game in which two 5-player teams compete to get the ball through the other player's hoop the most times in order to score the most points, or a huge ball used in the game.

    General Mechanics :

    - Basketball is a team sport. Two teams of five players each try to score by shooting a ball through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground. The game is played on a rectangular floor called the court, and there is a hoop at each end. The court is divided into two main sections by the mid-court line.

    Volleyball

    - Volleyball is a skill-based sport in which two teams compete to pass a ball over the net without it touching the ground or going out of bounds. When a team's opponent makes an error or fails to lawfully return the ball over the net, they get points

    General Mechanics :

    - Volleyball is a sport in which two teams of six players compete on a court separated by a net. The receiving side receives the right to serve when it wins a volley, and the players rotate one position clockwise. The serving team earns a point and the chance to continue serving when it wins a volley. On a serve, the ball must clear the net.

    Soccer

    - Soccer is a sport in which two teams of eleven players compete with a round ball. Players pass the ball to one another and attempt to score goals by kicking it into a big net.

    General Mechanics :

    - The purpose of the game is to score a goal by kicking or heading the ball into the goal of the other team. If the ball hits or crosses the sideline, the team that was not the last to touch it throws it back in. A central referee and two linesmen are in charge of the game.

    Importance Of Team Sports

    - Participation in team sports allows kids to make lasting friendships, develop communication skills, feel a sense of community, and learn to respect their teammates and coaches. Additionally, being part of a team teaches children the importance of being a responsible and reliable team member
